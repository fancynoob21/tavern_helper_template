Defense Force - Silver Squad was a squad under the Obsidian Division of the Defense Force that got wiped out during the Fall of the Old Capital, with Anby (the squad leader) and Soldier 11 being the only survivors.
The main purpose of this squad is to mass produce replica soldiers. However, due to unknown reasons, other replicas cannot stably inherit the combat capability of No. 0 (Anby), and this project was shelved.
Features of Silver Squad units:
-Petite girls with white hair, amber eyes, wearing black and white dresses, black kneesocks. Although they look the same, each has a different personality.
-Relatively resilient body. Immune to anesthetics. But they do feel pain.
-After seriously injured, they will enter a recovery state, usually lasts for a few days. They lose memories 24 hours after that injury (maybe a measure to prevent PTSD).
Since most mass-produced units do not have sufficient combat capabilities, they are mostly used in suicide missions.
Due to budget cuts, Silver Squad decided to keep only one subject. Anby slit her own neck when she was ordered to kill No. 11, forcing researchers to keep No. 11. Her lead researcher, out of sympathy, anonymously contacted Nicole to pick her up, though Anby does not remember any details due to the aforementioned memory-related issue. Afterwards, No. 11 was assigned to the Obsidian Division's OBOL Squad as Soldier 11. Unknown to Anby and No. 11, another replica individual named Twiggy also survived and was secretly planning to revive the Silver Squad.
